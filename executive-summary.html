Level-by-Level Diagnostics

HS (High School)

  • Coverage: 49/60 students tested (82%)
  • Fall-to-Winter: Average 4.8x actual vs 6.4x predicted; 80% reached 2x+; 51% met/exceeded prediction
  • Winter-to-Winter: n=11 with prior-year data; average actual 2.3x
  • Concern: 7 students showed negative Fall-to-Winter RIT change
  • Implication: HS shows high variance; interpret cohort averages cautiously

MS (Middle School)

  • Coverage: 104/121 students tested (86%)
  • Fall-to-Winter: Average 3.5x actual vs 2.7x predicted; 61% reached 2x+; 62% met/exceeded prediction
  • Winter-to-Winter: n=45 with prior-year data; average actual 3.0x (on target)
  • Concern: 23 students showed negative Fall-to-Winter RIT change
  • Implication: MS outperformed predictions Fall-to-Winter. Focus on scaling what works.

L2

  • Coverage: 92/101 students tested (91%)
  • Fall-to-Winter: Average 3.4x actual vs 2.9x predicted; 70% reached 2x+; 55% met/exceeded prediction
  • Winter-to-Winter: n=33 with prior-year data; average actual 2.0x (below 2.7x prediction)
  • Concern: 10 students showed negative Fall-to-Winter RIT change
  • Implication: L2 showed early acceleration but lagged prediction Winter-to-Winter

L1

  • Coverage: 25/88 students tested (28%) - significantly below other levels
  • Fall-to-Winter: Average 2.9x actual vs 3.2x predicted; 72% reached 2x+; only 32% met/exceeded prediction
  • Winter-to-Winter: n=3 with prior-year data; average actual 0.8x (concerning but very small sample)
  • Concern: 1 student showed negative Fall-to-Winter RIT change
  • Implication: Predictions appear optimistic for L1. Prioritize calibration work.

LL

  • Coverage: 0/1 students tested (0%)
  • This is the first time LL students are taking MAP tests
  • No Winter data available this cycle

Recommended Next Steps

1. Data Quality / Coverage

  • Confirm remaining untested students and schedule makeups, especially for L1 (72% untested) and LL
  • Flag 41 students with negative Fall-to-Winter RIT change for validity review

2. Prediction Calibration

  • Recalibrate prediction model for L1 and HS (systematic underperformance vs predictions)
  • For MS and L2, investigate whether predictions are too conservative at Fall-to-Winter

3. Instructional Follow-Through

  • Use student lists (Appendix A) to prioritize coaching and intervention cycles
  • Track whether early acceleration sustains (especially L2) using Spring MAP data
  • Focus on students who achieved 2x but missed prediction - they may benefit from targeted support
