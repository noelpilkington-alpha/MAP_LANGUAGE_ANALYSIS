What is a Writing Deep Dive?

These students have earned significant Writing XP (1,000+ XP) but are not showing expected MAP growth (<2x). The disconnect between effort and results suggests we need to investigate:

  • Is the Writing work being completed with quality, or just for completion?
  • Are students engaging meaningfully with feedback?
  • Is there a skill transfer gap between Writing practice and Language MAP content?
  • Are there external factors affecting test performance?

Writing Deep Dive Investigation Questions

  • Work Quality: Review actual writing submissions - are they thoughtful or rushed?
  • Feedback Engagement: Are students reading and applying feedback on their writing?
  • Time on Task: Compare XP to active minutes - is the ratio reasonable?
  • Writing vs. Language: Is there a disconnect between writing skills and Language MAP content areas?
  • Test Conditions: Were there testing anomalies (fatigue, distractions, illness)?
  • External Factors: Are there attendance, engagement, or personal issues affecting performance?
