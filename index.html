Methodology Note

Key analyses exclude High School (HS) students to eliminate grade-level inflation. At higher grade levels, NWEA projected growth norms decrease significantly (often to 1 RIT point), making it easier to achieve high growth multipliers. The non-HS analysis provides a more accurate picture of program effectiveness.
